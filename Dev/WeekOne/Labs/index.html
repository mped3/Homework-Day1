Margherita Pizza

This is how you successfully make a delicious margherita pizza originated from Naples, Italy. This recipe is quick and easy, it only requires a few ingredients. Fun way to bond with family, and make a delicious homeade pizza.

Ingredients :

  • 1lb pizza dough
  • 28oz can whole San Marzano tomatoes
  • 12oz fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil
  • Optional: Extra-virgin olive oil, crushed red pepper flakes, garlic powder

Instructions :

  1. Preheat oven to 500*F & place oven rack on third level closes to the top. Prepare the dough.
  2. Pour canned tomatoes into bowl, chop up the tomatoes, and spread 3/4 cup of tomato sauce onto the dough.
  3. Cut fesh mozzarella and distribute all over the pizza mostly towards the center.
  4. Bake pizza until golden for 10-12 min
  5. Top pizza with granishes and let it cool down
  6. ENJOY!
